Meet Our Chefs

KARL DE GUZMAN

PRESIDENT & CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER

BRINGING HIS 17 YEARS OF OPERATIONS EXPERIENCE, KARL DE GUZMAN HAS BEEN LIVING IN DUBAI SINCE 2006. HE BRINGS TO THE TABLE HIS EXPERTISE IN PROJECT MANAGEMENT AND CUSTOMER SERVICE AS THE PRESIDENT AND COO OF ICHIRYU RAMEN HOUSE. FOCUSING ON CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE, KARL LEADS THE OPERATIONS GROUP THAT ENSURES THAT EVERY CUSTOMER IS GIVEN TOP-NOTCH SERVICE AND SUPPORT AT ALL TIMES

RAUL DE GUZMAN

EXECUTIVE CHEF & CHIEF PROCUREMENT OFFICER

ICHIRYU RAMEN HOUSE IS LED BY THE HEAD CHEF AND CHIEF PROCUREMENT OFFICER, RAUL “CHEF TOURU” DE GUZMAN. HE GREW UP AND STUDIED IN JAPAN. HIS PASSION FOR JAPANESE FOOD AND SPECIALIZING IN RAMEN STARTED AS EARLY AS 1989. HE EMBODIES “NANA KOROBI YA OKI” OR “FALL SEVEN TIMES, GET UP EIGHT.” HE BELIEVES THAT LIFE IS ABOUT NEVER GIVING UP ON ONESELF AND HAVING THE COURAGE TO PUSH ON NO MATTER WHAT OBSTACLES COME HIS WAY.

NICOLAI DE GUZMAN

CHAIRMAN & CHIEF DESIGN OFFICER

THE YOUNGEST, NICOLAI DE GUZMAN, IS THE CHAIRMAN AND CHIEF DESIGN OFFICER (CDO). HE BRINGS WITH HIM HIS 16 YEARS OF EXPERIENCE AS A STUDIO DIRECTOR FOR AN EXPERIENTIAL AGENCY IN THE UAE.
